Description

Escape Locke Manor by discovering hidden objects, hunting for clues, solving puzzles, and ultimately cracking the final riddle of the deranged cult whom you call family.

History

The game had its inception when Juan and Maciej had to come up with something for an assignment at university.

Features

  • An atmospheric escape room game filled with mystery and intrigue.
  • Solve puzzles, find clues, and learn dark family secrets.
  • Play multiplayer with up to 4 friends, or play solo!
  • Explore a beautifully crafted Victorian manor dripping with the occult.
